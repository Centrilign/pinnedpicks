1. Introduction

Welcome to PinnedPicks™ ("we," "our," or "us"), a curated product discovery platform operated by Centralyze. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information related to your use of our website. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the site.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last updated" date. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates.

2. Information We Collect

PinnedPicks™ does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or payment details. However, we use third party tracking scripts and analytics platforms to better understand how visitors interact with our website.

The only data stored locally on your device is your theme preference (light or dark mode) and your saved/bookmarked product pins. These preferences are stored using your browser's localStorage feature. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Your hosting provider and CDN services may independently process standard technical data such as IP addresses, browser information, request timestamps, and security-related diagnostics as part of normal website infrastructure operations. This processing is governed by their respective privacy policies and infrastructure practices.

3. Google Analytics and Tag Manager

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Tag Manager to collect anonymous user data, including page views, scroll depth, session duration, and outbound link clicks. This data helps us improve platform performance and curated user experiences. Google may use cookies or identifiers to collect this technical information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information stored via browser localStorage is used solely to remember your display preferences and saved pins between visits. Standard technical website interactions and analytics tracking data are utilized to measure traffic patterns, optimize performance, and improve the curated browsing experience.

5. Affiliate Links and Third-Party Websites

Our website contains affiliate links to third-party websites including Shopee, SHEIN, and Amazon. When you click these links and make purchases, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we do not accept responsibility or liability for their content or privacy practices.

Product pricing is displayed on the respective third-party marketplace. PinnedPicks™ does not guarantee real-time or accurate pricing at the moment of viewing. Users should always verify the final price on the destination platform before making any purchasing decisions.

We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit. Clicking affiliate links means you are leaving PinnedPicks™ and entering a third-party platform subject to that platform's own terms and conditions.

6. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage feature to remember certain user-device preferences and actions, including your selected theme preference (light or dark mode) and your saved/bookmarked product pins. This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties. You may clear this data at any time through your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the website.

7. Feedback Forms and External Services

PinnedPicks™ may provide optional feedback or contact forms powered by third-party services such as Google Forms. Any information voluntarily submitted through these services is processed according to the respective provider's privacy policy and is not stored directly by PinnedPicks™ infrastructure.

Users should avoid submitting sensitive personal, financial, or confidential information through public feedback forms.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
