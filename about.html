Who We Are

PinnedPicks™ is a curated product discovery platform founded by Centralyze on May 4, 2026. It helps people find useful, trending, and aesthetically pleasing products from trusted online marketplaces, all in one place.

We carefully organize and showcase products across categories like fashion, tech, lifestyle, home finds, beauty, accessories, and everyday essentials to make online shopping easier, faster, and more inspiring.

Through visual content, curated collections, and affiliate partnerships with platforms like Shopee, SHEIN, and Amazon, PinnedPicks™ connects users to products they may genuinely love while helping them discover new brands, deals, and viral finds across the internet.

What We Do

  • Curate trending and practical products
  • Create visual shopping inspiration through Pinterest and web content
  • Organize products into easy-to-browse collections
  • Help users discover affordable, stylish, and useful finds
  • Partner with trusted ecommerce platforms through affiliate programs

Our Goal

PinnedPicks™ aims to become a modern product discovery hub where people can explore curated online finds without endlessly searching across multiple shopping apps and websites.

Whether you're looking for room upgrades, fashion inspiration, everyday tech, beauty products, or lifestyle essentials, PinnedPicks™ helps simplify the search by bringing the best picks together in one place.

Platform Development & Community Feedback

PinnedPicks™ is continuously evolving to improve product discovery, browsing experience, platform performance, and overall usability across devices.

We actively refine features, optimize interface responsiveness, and expand platform functionality based on automated analytics performance data, real user experiences, and community insights.

Feedback, suggestions, and usability recommendations are highly valued. If you encounter issues or have ideas that could improve the platform, we’d love to hear from you.
